How to Play

  • Rotate: A / D
  • Throttle: W / S (raises NI; coasting lowers NI)
  • Passive Arc width: Q / E (changes coverage + performance)
  • Active Ping: Space (shows crisp contacts; cooldown bar under HUD)
  • Dark Run: hold Shift (lower NI; active may be limited)

Objective: Track the prey using Passive + breadcrumbs, then Active-ping when very close. Prey escapes at the green gate before the timer runs out.

Visuals
  • Rings: inner = Ambient, middle = Passive (base), thick blue = Passive (effective), outer = Active
  • Arc wedge: your Passive field-of-view; only targets inside can return hits
  • Breadcrumbs: blue = passive (approximate), orange = decoys (confirm with Active)
  • Red circles = reveal bubbles: small (Passive), large (Active)
Scanning Layers
  • Ambient (always on): short range hints; ignores Arc
  • Passive (Arc-directed): returns fuzzy positions inside the wedge
    Narrower Arc = longer passive range and higher accuracy, with a smaller reveal pulse. Wider Arc = more coverage, worse accuracy, larger pulse.
  • Active (Ping): exact positions for a moment; big reveal pulse; NI spikes; obeys cooldown

Tips: Search wide, then narrow the Arc and sweep to triangulate; ping to confirm. Watch NI and reveal bubbles to manage risk.
